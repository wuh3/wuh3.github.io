6 DOF Robot Arm Control and Dynamics

As the final project of my Robot Dynamics & Control course, Allen and I worked as a group of two and compiled a MATLAB script which generates the forward and inverse kinematics of a S12 robot. Given 4 poses in 3-dimensional space, we calculated all the velocity and position singularities and all possible inverse kinematics solutions. Then, we controlled the robot with inverse dynamics control, PD feedback controller, and a model-based tracking controller. Finally, we demonstrated all the singularities and inverse kinematic solutions using the built-in software of the robot.
